Sustainability

We know that the choices we make today have a profound impact on the future of our planet.

We take sustainability seriously, from sourcing our ingredients all the way to how our products are displayed on the shelves.

We work closely with our farmers to ensure that we only use the freshest and most sustainable ingredients. Our farmers carefully select ingredients that would have otherwise been wasted, helping to reduce food waste and make a positive difference in the world. We source our ingredients from trusted partners who share our commitment to sustainability, choosing suppliers who are located as close to us as possible to decrease the distance that needs to be crossed for certain ingredients.

Nurturing Sustainability and Flavorful Traditions for a Lasting Impact

Our commitment to sustainability doesn't stop there. We believe in traditional cooking techniques, bringing out the vibrant flavors of our ingredients while saying no to any artificial flavorings, colors, and preservatives. We try our best to minimise our impact on the environment by using eco-friendly packaging materials as much as possible.

By choosing Harvie, you're making a choice to support a company that is committed to truly making a positive and lasting impact.
